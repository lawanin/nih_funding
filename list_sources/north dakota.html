Medical School

The UND School of Medicine & Health Sciences is the only medical school in North Dakota. The four-year M.D. degree has been offered since 1976.

78 

Medical students admitted each year

7 

Medical students partnering with our Indians Into Medicine (INMED) program

Administration

The medical side of UND's School of Medicine & Health Sciences is administered by the School's Dean, Joshua Wynne, M.D., M.B.A., M.P.H., along with the following Associate Deans.

This team coordinates with each of the School's department and program chairs, associate and assistant deans, and faculty to maintain the policies and programs that give shape to one of the nation's leading medical schools focusing on not only the primary care specialties but the health sciences.
