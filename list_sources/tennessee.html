        Campuses and Departments

        Four locations constitute the University of Tennessee Health Science Center College of Medicine. The largest of the locations is Memphis. The first two years of medical student training are spent on the Memphis campus with a large portion of the clinical training being performed in Memphis-affiliated hospitals. Memphis is also the site of the college's largest Graduate Medical Education (GME), research, and clinical programs.
