UI COM at Chicago Departments and Centers

University of Illinois College of Medicine at Chicago offers medical education, residencies and graduate education in twenty-four departments. From Anatomy and Cell Biology to urology, each department boasts top faculty and groundbreaking research that attract some of the best scientists and physicians in the country. The Chicago campus also takes pride in its world-renowned Centers which each focus on a clearly defined mission and serve to broaden our ability to accomplish goals which would be more difficult to tackle within the confines of a single department.
