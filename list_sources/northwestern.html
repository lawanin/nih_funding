About

The Basic Science Administration (BSA) group supports the basic science academic departments of Biochemistry and Molecular Genetics, Cell & Developmental Biology, Microbiology-Immunology, Neuroscience, and Pharmacology.

We also support the:

  • Simpson Querrey Institute for Epigenetics
  • Center for Autism and Neurodevelopment
  • Center for Genetic Medicine
  • Center for Structural Genomics of Infectious Diseases
  • Center for Translational Pain Research

And several Core Facilities:

  • Behavioral Phenotyping Core
  • Center for Advanced Microscopy
  • NUgene Project
  • NUSeq
  • Transgenic and Targeted Mutagenesis Laboratory
