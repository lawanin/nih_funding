Academic Units

In medical education, we marvel at the human body as both magnificent and complex.  At MSU College of Human Medicine, we teach our students to focus on the individual needs of the patient, while developing an understanding of medical science and medicine's place in society.  Our faculty emphasize this patient-centered philosophy in their approach to medical education and caring for patients.
