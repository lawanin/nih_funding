  2. Oct 25 Orthopaedic Surgery Journal Club5:30pm

    Orthopaedic Surgery Journal Club

    Monday, October 25th, 2021

    05:30 PM - 07:00 PM

    UConn Health
    WEBEX

    Speaker: Dr. Ran Schwartzkopf, Dr. Augustus Mazzocca, Dr. Stephen Davis & Dr. Isaac Moss
    Date: October 25, 2021
    Time: 5:30pm-7:00pm
    Location: Virtual - WEBEX

    Target Audience: Faculty, staff, and trainees at the UConn Health Center

    Learning Objectives: Participants will (be able to):
    1. Determine if the use of tranexamic acid (TXA) is safe in in patients undergoing total joint arthroplasty with history of coronary artery disease or coronary stents.
    2. Evaluate patient outcomes when using Bridging Allograft Reconstruction versus Maximal Repair for Massive Rotator Cuff tear treatment.
    3. Assess the safety and feasibility to use monitored anesthesia care with local anesthetic for treatment of intertrochanteric hip fractures in patients with severe medical comorbidities.
    4. Analyze the correlation between thoracolumbar imbalance and outcomes when performing corrective cervical deformity.

    Contact Information: Michelle Ambrosio mambrosio@uchc.edu
