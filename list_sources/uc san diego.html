Departments at UC San Diego School of Medicine

Page Content

At UC San Diego School of Medicine, our departments are not silos they are bridges between our research laboratories and our hospitals and clinics.

We collaborate across departments, with other disciplines across campus and with our colleagues at neighboring institutions to deliver outstanding patient care today, educate the doctors and scientists of tomorrow, and drive the translational research that will transform the future of health care for all.

  • Cellular and Molecular Medicine
  • Dermatology
