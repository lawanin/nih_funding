School of Medicine Departments

The primary mission at the Tulane University School of Medicine is to conduct the highest quality departmental educational programs, which will result in academically and clinically prepared physicians, biomedical scientists and public health professionals. These departments and programs will provide the education needed for Tulane graduates to satisfy regional, national and international health needs.
