Northwest Campus

This regional campus is home to three tracks for our medical students: the three-year Primary Care Scholars M.D. track, the traditional four-year M.D. track, and the option to complete the first and second years of medical school at UAMS Little Rock and the third and fourth years at UAMS Northwest in Fayetteville. This campus also houses Internal Medicine and Family Medicine residencies.
