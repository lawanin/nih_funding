UNLV, Home of Las Vegas' Top Doctors

The Kirk Kerkorian School of Medicine at UNLV, in addition to educating future medical providers, is focused on making Southern Nevada a healthier place to live and work. This is seen in the education, community service, research, and patient care that are integral to our mission. These efforts have resulted in many of our faculty being recognized as top doctors in their fields.
