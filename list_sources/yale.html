Departments at Yale School of Medicine

Our more than 11,000 faculty and staff members are the heart of the Yale School of Medicine. The school’s reputation as a leading center for biomedical research, advanced clinical care and inspired medical education owes much to their dedication and hard work.

Their commitment, compassion and ceaseless energy are an inspiration to students and colleagues alike.
